About Us

Welcome to Pickle & Platter — where tradition meets taste.

We are a homegrown brand dedicated to crafting authentic, homemade Indian achaars, chutneys, and healthy bites using traditional recipes passed down through generations. Every jar we prepare carries the essence of homemade goodness, natural ingredients, and rich flavors.

Based in Bhopal, we focus on small-batch production to ensure quality, freshness, and hygiene. Our products are made without artificial preservatives and are prepared with care, just like they are made in Indian households.

From tangy mango pickles to spicy chutneys and nutritious laddoos, we bring you flavors that remind you of home.

Our Mission: To deliver authentic, high-quality homemade food products that celebrate Indian traditions.

Our Vision: To become a trusted name for homemade delicacies across India.
