Refund Policy

Due to the nature of food products, we do not accept returns once the product is delivered.

However, refunds or replacements may be considered under the following conditions:

  • Damaged or leaked product upon delivery
  • Incorrect item received

To request a refund or replacement, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with proof (images/videos).

Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days.
